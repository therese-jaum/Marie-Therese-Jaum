About Me

Designing with Purpose

I'm Marie Therese Nalam Jaum, a graphic designer focused on brand identity, editorial design, and visual storytelling. I turn complex ideas into powerful visual narratives through strategic thinking and confident creative execution across print, digital, and multimedia design.
